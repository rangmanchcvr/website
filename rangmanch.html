A stage built

Rangmanch began as a shared dream among a handful of students and our faculty coordinator, united by the belief that every story deserved a stage. A decade later, it stands as one of the college's most celebrated and dynamic clubs, carrying forward a legacy of creativity, passion, and the spirit of theatre.
